Ayurveda

Ayurveda, dating back 5000-10000 years is the oldest and most holistic medical system available on the planet today. The word Ayurveda comes from the Sanskrit, “Ayur” and “veda” which means the Science of life. Ayurveda is the ancient wisdom of life revealed to the congregation of enlightened sages at the foot hills of Himalayas. It is the extraordinary mind -body medicine of India, a tremendous resource for bringing holiness at all levels of our existence.

Ayurveda focus on individual’s holistic health rather than mere physical body. It works on the principle that perfect health condition is obtained through the psychosomatic integration in a person. Ayurveda places great emphasis on prevention and encourages maintaining health by paying close attention to balance in one’s life through right thinking, diet, lifestyle and use of herbs. It provides us with unique herbal health remedies and purificatory procedures that is panchakarma which corrects imbalance of humors or doshas namely, vata pitta and kapha for physical,mental and spiritual well being.
